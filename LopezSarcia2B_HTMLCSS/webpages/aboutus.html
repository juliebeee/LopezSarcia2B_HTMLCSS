This blog is aimed at showing you the places, things and events that you should not miss when you are in Marinduque. Let us be your reference when you open the site for Tres Reyes Islands; we will be full of information and guide for you!

We will appreciate it if you will regularly drop by and visit our many pages and invite your friends and relatives to do so as well. We update this website regularly, we are sure you will find something new when you visit. Hope to see you again soon!
